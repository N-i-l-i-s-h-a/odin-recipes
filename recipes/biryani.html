Description:

A flavorful South Asian rice dish made with fragrant spices, marinated chicken, and basmati rice‚Äîlayered and slow-cooked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 mins)
  • 500g chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp biryani masala (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro and mint
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and half the garam masala for 1 hour.
  2. Fry onions until golden. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  4. In a separate pot, parboil rice with salt. Drain.
  5. Layer chicken and rice. Sprinkle fried onions, cilantro, mint, and garam masala.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15‚Äì20 minutes.
  7. Fluff gently and serve with raita or salad.
