🍰 Cheesecake Recipe

Description:

A rich and creamy baked cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust, perfect for any dessert lover.

Ingredients:

  • Crust:
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

  • Filling:
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix crust ingredients and press into a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, vanilla, and eggs one at a time. Mix in sour cream.
  4. Pour filling over crust and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for 55–60 minutes. Turn off oven, crack door, and let sit for 1 hour.
  6. Chill for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
