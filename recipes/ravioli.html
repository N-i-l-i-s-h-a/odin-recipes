Description:

Classic Italian pasta pillows filled with ricotta and spinach, served with a light tomato sauce. Perfect for a hearty and elegant meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked spinach (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, eggs, and olive oil into a dough.
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minutes, then let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. In another bowl, combine ricotta, spinach, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roll out the dough thinly and place small amounts of filling on one side.
  5. Seal with another layer of dough and cut into squares.
  6. Cut into ravioli squares. Boil in salted water for 3â€“4 minutes until they float.
  7. Serve hot with warmed tomato sauce and fresh basil if desired.
